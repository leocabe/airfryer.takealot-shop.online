Kitchen Power Combo — Air Fryer & Kenwood Blender Set

Upgrade your kitchen experience with the perfect combination of convenience, performance, and healthy cooking.

This powerful kitchen combo brings together the versatility of the Kenwood Blender and the efficiency of a modern Air Fryer, giving you everything you need to prepare delicious meals, snacks, and drinks with ease.

Designed for everyday cooking, this bundle allows you to blend, grind, fry, bake, and crisp your favorite foods while saving time and promoting a healthier lifestyle.

  • LCD touch screen control.
  • Adjustable temperature & timer.
  • Non-stick removable basket.
  • Easy cleaning design.
  • Healthy low-oil cooking.
  • Power rating: 1800W–2400W.

  • Ingredient opening on lid.
